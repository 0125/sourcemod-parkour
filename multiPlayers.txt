	L4DToolZ
		https://forums.alliedmods.net/showthread.php?t=93600?t=93600
			helper thread: https://forums.alliedmods.net/showthread.php?t=232367
	L4DToolZ Config
		sv_maxplayers "whateveryouwant"
		sv_visiblemaxplayers "-1"
		sv_removehumanlimit "1"
		sv_force_unreserved "1"
		note
			if you want to use connecting though lobby and connecting through the
			+connect command you need another mod that removes the lobby reservation.
